CareerAlly!

CareerAlly helps job seekers find support through mentorships tailored to support their career goals and they can also find jobs to apply to after their mentors set them up for success.

Overview

What is CareerAlly app?
Users need an app that helps them find mentorship and jobs. They also need to find reliable resources that help them find their dream job and support their long-term career goals.
My Role
UX Researchers and UX Designers
Team
Reed Goodman, Jennifer Villagomez, Bhargavi Marathe, Eriny Ibrahim.
Tools
Trello, Figma, Miro, AdobeXD, Adobe Illustrator

Problem

Problem Statement

We believe creating a more inclusive job search platform that has one place to find mentors and openings for job seekers will achieve success in finding job opportunities that are better suited to their skillset and better preparation in elevating long-term career goals.

Users are not sure where to find well trusted resources instead of scams to search for jobs and there is also difficulty finding community and support for those who are trying to make a career transition.

Solution

Solution

CareerAlly helps job seekers find support through mentorships tailored to support their career goals and they can also find jobs to apply to after their mentors set them up for success.

User Research

Research Objective

Understand user needs and expectations by gaining insights into the preferences and challenges faced by job seekers searching for mentorship.

Identify pain points with existing job search platforms and explore the channels through which mentors and mentees connect.

Collect feedback on potential improvements so we can create a user-centric job search app that empowers job seekers with valuable mentorship support.
