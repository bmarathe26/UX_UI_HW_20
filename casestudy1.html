Why We Start This Project & Goal

Why we start?

The Gamers Outreach Redesign project was initiated to address critical issues within the gaming community. Our mission was to optimize the donation process, inspire more charity streams, increase donation amounts, and build trust among the gaming community. Through user interviews, extensive research, and a rigorous testing process, we sought to create an optimal solution that would revolutionize gaming philanthropy.

Problem statement & Challenges

Problem

We needed to enhance the Gamers Outreach website in order to optimize the donation process, encourage more charity streams, increase donation amounts, and foster positive reviews from, and thus build the trust of, the gaming community.

Response of Qualitative & Quantitative Research

User Research

Initial user research was needed in order to devise the most optimal solution for this problem, and our team utilized both primary and secondary means of conducting research. We wrote three interview scripts; for streamers, donors, and hospital representatives, and got to work recruiting and interviewing those who met each demographic. We supplemented with secondary research when we could not find enough and were not able to get in contact with Gamers Outreach representatives.
